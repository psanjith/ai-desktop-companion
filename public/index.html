❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Yes! Luren is open-source and completely free. You can download, install, and use it on your devices at no cost.

Does it require an internet connection?

The frontend (desktop app) runs locally, but the AI backend requires connectivity to call the LLM service (Groq, OpenAI, or local Ollama). Once responses are received, everything runs offline.

What data does Luren collect?

Luren stores conversation history locally on your device. Depending on your LLM provider (Groq, OpenAI), check their privacy policies. You can delete all local data anytime.

Can I customize the AI personality?

Yes! Edit the personalities.json file to change tone, energy, humor, and emotes. Create your own character or tweak Luna and Ren to your preference.

Does it support auto-updates?

Yes. When you launch Luren, it checks for updates automatically. If available, you'll be prompted to update.

What AI services are supported?

Groq (fastest, free tier available), OpenAI, and local Ollama. Configure in settings or config.json.

How do I set up a keyboard shortcut?

On macOS, use the Shortcuts app to trigger the app with Shift+Space. Windows/Linux: configure via your OS's hotkey settings or use the app's built-in shortcut settings.

Can I contribute or report bugs?

Absolutely! Visit our GitHub to report issues, suggest features, or contribute code.
