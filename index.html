❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Luren free?

Yes! Luren is completely free and open-source. Download, install, and use it on your devices at no cost.

Does it need internet?

The desktop app runs locally, but needs internet to connect to your AI provider (Groq, OpenAI, or local Ollama). Once responses arrive, everything else is offline.

Is my data private?

Yes. Conversations are stored locally on your device. You can delete all data anytime. Your LLM provider (Groq/OpenAI) may log requests—check their privacy policy.

Can I customize Luren?

Absolutely! Edit personalities.json to change tone, humor, emotes, and behavior. Create your own character or modify Luna and Ren.

Does it auto-update?

Yes. Luren checks for new versions on startup. When available, you'll get a prompt to update with one click.

Which AI services work?

Groq (fastest & free-tier friendly), OpenAI, and local Ollama. Configure in settings or config.json.

Found a bug or have an idea?

We'd love to hear from you! Visit GitHub to report issues or suggest features.

Can I contribute?

Yes! The project is open-source. Fork the repo, make improvements, and submit a pull request. All contributions welcome.
